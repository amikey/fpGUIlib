Then go to:
=> https://github.com/graemeg/fpGUI/tree/develop
and click on "Download ZIP".

Unzip the folder.

Add all the Fu and Fi needed by fpGUI in fpc.cfg or use fpc patch in /patch (read readme.txt before apply patch please)

Then compile fpGUI.pas from /src
